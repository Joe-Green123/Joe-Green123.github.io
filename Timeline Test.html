Our Process

Sign up Get in touch with our friendly sales team to place your order

Our price list can be found here

A deposit of 50% is needed on order, with the remaining balance due on site launch
Consult

Our team will send you a simple questionnaire to get an idea of how you want your website to be

Design

Our design team will then make a mock up of your website so you know how it will look

We allow as many revisions as needed to get your website perfect

Development

Once the look of the website is agreed upon, our team will begin coding

At this stage you will provide us with the content (words, pictures etc) for the website

Launch

Once the website is finished it will go live!

If you already have a domain name we will assist you with the domain configuration

If you do not own a domain, we will assist you in acquiring one at no extra cost

Support Our work is not finished when your website goes live

We are happy to assist you in making updates that you can't or do not wish to do via your CMS at an hourly rate

We also offer SEO (Search Engine Optimisation) services
